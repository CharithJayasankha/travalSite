Places

Sigiriya

Explore Sigiriya, the iconic Lion Rock fortress that towers 200 meters above the plains. This UNESCO World Heritage site features ancient frescoes, a pair of giant lion's paws, and the remnants of a 5th-century palace. Climb to the top for breathtaking views and a glimpse into Sri Lanka's rich history.

Nine Arch Bridge

It is located in Demodara, between Ella and Demodara railway stations. The surrounding area has seen a steady increase of tourism due to the bridge's architectural ingenuity and the profuse greenery in the nearby hillsides.

Temple of the Tooth

Visit the Temple of the Tooth, one of Sri Lanka's most sacred Buddhist sites, located in the heart of Kandy. This revered temple houses a tooth relic of Lord Buddha, attracting pilgrims from around the world. The stunning architecture and serene surroundings make it a must-visit for anyone seeking to experience Sri Lanka's rich spiritual heritage.

Galle Fort

Explore Galle Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage site built by the Portuguese in the 16th century and later expanded by the Dutch. This historic fort features charming streets, colonial-era buildings, and stunning ocean views.
